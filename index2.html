About us

With an aim to equip each student in the KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS and VALUES for enhancing ones attitude and personality we- a group of academicians, value-educators, spiritualists, corporates and students came together and formed the VOICE (Vedic Oasis of Inspiration, Culture and Education) club which has now been successfully functioning for over a decade in various premier educational institutes in India and a few in abroad.

The VOICE club is one of the most prevalent and active student community involved in conducting various activities aimed at overall student development.
